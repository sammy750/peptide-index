The [Wolverine Stack](/peptides/wolverine-stack/) is a dual-peptide research formulation containing **BPC-157 (10mg) and TB-500 (10mg)** — 20mg total — supplied as a pre-configured laboratory format. Standardising the two peptides in one vial gives researchers a consistent composition and removes variability from solution preparation, supporting reproducible handling and uniform exposure across non-clinical workflows.

> **Research use only.** The Wolverine Stack is supplied strictly for in-vitro laboratory research. This page does not describe dosing, administration, or use in humans or animals, and makes no therapeutic claims.

## The two components

- **[BPC-157](/peptides/bpc-157/)** — studied as a probe for peptide-mediated signalling and cellular-response mechanisms.
- **[TB-500](/peptides/tb-500/)** — a thymosin beta-4 fragment studied for actin dynamics and cytoskeletal organisation.

## Why study them as a set

The combined system lets researchers examine **dual-peptide interactions** and observe coordinated or independent pathway activity within a single solution matrix — for example, comparing cellular signalling and structural responses side by side under standardised conditions.

Typical research applications include cellular signalling and receptor-interaction studies, cytoskeletal modelling related to actin dynamics, peptide-stability analysis, and combined-peptide interaction studies.

## How it is supplied

Supplied as a vial with a Certificate of Analysis, stored refrigerated at 2–8°C. The components are also available individually: [BPC-157](/peptides/bpc-157/) and [TB-500](/peptides/tb-500/).
