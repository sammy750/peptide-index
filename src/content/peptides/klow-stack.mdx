The [Klow Stack](/peptides/klow-stack/) is a four-peptide research formulation containing **GHK-Cu (50mg), KPV (10mg), BPC-157 (10mg) and TB-500 (10mg)** — 80mg total. Presenting four peptides with distinct mechanisms in one vial lets researchers investigate combined activity across multiple signalling pathways simultaneously, under standardised conditions.

> **Research use only.** The Klow Stack is supplied strictly for in-vitro laboratory research. This page does not describe dosing, administration, or use in humans or animals, and makes no therapeutic claims.

## The four components

- **[GHK-Cu](/peptides/ghk-cu/)** — copper-peptide complex studied for copper-dependent signalling.
- **[KPV](/peptides/kpv/)** — α-MSH (11-13) tripeptide studied for melanocortin-linked and inflammatory signalling.
- **[BPC-157](/peptides/bpc-157/)** — probe for peptide-mediated signalling mechanisms.
- **[TB-500](/peptides/tb-500/)** — thymosin beta-4 fragment studied for actin/cytoskeletal dynamics.

## Why study them as a set

The four-peptide system supports exploration of **convergent and parallel signalling** across copper-dependent, melanocortin-linked and cytoskeletal pathways within one experimental matrix — and comparison of individual versus combined activity.

## How it is supplied

Supplied as a vial with a Certificate of Analysis, stored refrigerated at 2–8°C. The components are available individually too.
