PT-141 (bremelanotide) is a synthetic cyclic heptapeptide and an analogue of the melanocortin peptide α-MSH. In research it is studied as a non-selective melanocortin-receptor agonist. Its cyclic, modified structure has no standard one-letter residue string, so no sequence fingerprint is shown.
